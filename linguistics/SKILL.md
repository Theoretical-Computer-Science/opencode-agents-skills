## Key Concepts

### Language Components
- **Phonetics**: Sound production and perception
- **Phonology**: Sound patterns and rules
- **Morphology**: Word formation
- **Syntax**: Sentence structure
- **Semantics**: Meaning
- **Pragmatics**: Language use in context

### Key Linguistic Theories
- **Chomsky**: Transformational grammar, universal grammar
- **Saussure**: Signifier/signified, synchronic/diachronic
- **Austin/Searle**: Speech act theory
- **Grice**: Conversational implicature
- **Halliday**: Systemic functional grammar

### Language Typology
- Word order (SVO, SOV, VSO...)
- Morphological typology (analytic, synthetic, polysynthetic)
- Case systems
- Gender and number
